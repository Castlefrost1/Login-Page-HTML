About Klinika

The Hospital That cares.

Overview

Klinika is a private, trust-managed, autonomous, hospital. It is a multidisciplinary, 480-bed, tertiary care hospital. It houses several wards, operation theatres, ICUs, laboratories, utility services, specialties and super specialties, including one of the best blood banks in the country, catering to the entire state of Indonesia.
